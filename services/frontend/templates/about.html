About me

education, work, interests

That's me

My name is Philipp, I am 28 years old and currently live in Frankfurt am Main. I was born and raised in Southern Germany, which is certainly the most beautiful area in the country with the most delicious food.

Interests

This is an area that I have to update regularly, as my interests are always changing. At the moment I am very interested in music, especially the guitar. I own an Ibanez AW54OPN. Songs I play at the moment are for example river flows in you (Yiruma), slow cheetah (RHCP), i took a pill in ibiza (Mike Posner), let her go (Passenger), perfect (Ed Sheeran), wish you were (Pink Floyd). I'm also a passionate cook, like to go running, keep in shape, am interested in stocks and investments, and enjoy working on side projects in my spare time.

Background

After graduating from high school, I began my educational journey by studying international management at Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences. I then went on to get my master's degree in economics at the University of Ulm, majoring in finance and computer science. I particularly enjoyed my experiences abroad in Mexico, Malaysia and China. I fell in love with Shanghai, the culture, the people and all the little dumplings.

Work experience

My first major work experience was in the project management and logistics department for B. Braun, which is a German healthcare company. I quickly realized my enthusiasm for numbers, patterns and models and wanted to gain more experience in the financial sector. In the following years, I worked at Allianz Technology SE in the financial department and at Allianz Global Investors in a team that's best described as being the interface between portfolio management and retirement products. After graduating I decided to combine my roots in the asset management and finance sector with my enthusiasm for Python, databases and machine learning. Currently I am employed as an Analytics Trainee in the International Talent Programme at ING Germany. For me, the trainee program is the perfect career start, as working in different departments gives me the best possible overview of data science within the banking sector.
